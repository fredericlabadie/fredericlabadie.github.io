Work · Adyen Product Data

Six cases, ordered around product data systems.

This selection is built for the Senior Product Data Analyst role on Adyen Checkout. The ordering starts with scalable systems and source-of-truth delivery, then moves into product telemetry, engineering partnership, automation, and standards adoption.

The cases remain existing portfolio evidence. They are reframed only where the fit is clear: e-commerce analytics, production data quality, automated reporting, stakeholder alignment, and durable data foundations.

Evidence paths

Start where the role starts: ownership and scale.

The role brief asks for senior ownership of complex initiatives, scalable data solutions, compelling data storytelling, standards that become reusable, and influence across engineering and business teams. The evidence below follows that order.

Path 01 · Scalable data systems BigQuery · architecture · cost engineering

Three raw scans to one. Fifteen CTEs to zero. ~50% cost reduction.

A second-pass audit and architectural redesign of a GA4-to-BigQuery pipeline. The important move was not tuning a query; it was changing the shape of the system so the expensive thing was computed once and reused.

Adyen fit · Simple and scalable solutions to complex problems; ownership of technical direction; data architecture that makes downstream analysis and reporting easier to trust.

BigQuerySQLDAG redesignCost engineering
Path 02 · Source of truth and adoption BigQuery · Dataform · Looker Studio

The reporting layer survived the people who built it.

A reporting build that replaced recurring manual report pulls and survived client-team turnover because documentation, training, and operating practices were designed into the system.

Adyen fit · Central source of truth, stakeholder enablement, and decision surfaces that continue working after the original builder leaves the room.

DataformLooker StudioDocumentationEnablement
Path 03 · Product telemetry foundations Mobile app · GA4 · consent

From "what should we measure?" to 30+ shipped tickets.

Greenfield mobile UX telemetry across iOS and Android: event definitions, schema design, implementation tickets, QA, cross-platform parity, and consent governance before launch.

Adyen fit · The product-data foundation underneath conversion analytics, feature adoption, and trusted experimentation.

GA4Firebase SDKCross-platform parityConsent management
Path 04 · Engineering partnership Segment SDK · anomaly detection

A silent iOS data-loss bug, traced to the initialisation sequence.

A systematic iOS undercount looked like platform behaviour until the implementation path showed a silent cold-launch event-loss issue in the SDK initialisation sequence.

Adyen fit · Product data credibility depends on engineering partnership, runtime validation, and the willingness to challenge numbers that look clean but are wrong.

SegmentSDK debuggingParity analysisData quality
Path 05 · Automation and decision surfaces Claude API · Amplitude · alert triage

Designing AI workflows that do not outsource the thinking.

A production alert-triage workflow compressed 230 raw alerts to 39 posts while preserving human accountability and surfacing one critical feature failure that human-only review had missed.

Adyen fit · Automation that improves the decision surface without delegating judgement to the model.

Claude APIAmplitudeAlert triageHuman-AI workflow

Introducing A/B testing as a leadership practice.

Five years building marketing analytics from scratch across a 280-location franchise, including the warehouse foundation, customer identity model, and the adoption of A/B testing as a leadership decision practice.

Adyen fit · Standards become real when leadership, product, and commercial teams change how they ask for evidence.
