About · Adyen Product Data

Senior analytics work, pointed at product decisions.

I am a data and analytics leader based in Amsterdam with 14 years of experience across product analytics, digital analytics, marketing analytics, data architecture, and stakeholder enablement. The common thread is translating unclear business or product questions into data systems that people can trust and use.

For Adyen, the relevant part is not domain equivalence. Payments infrastructure would be new territory. The relevant part is the operating pattern: own the problem, define the data model, build the scalable solution, align stakeholders around a shared source of truth, and make the result usable by product, engineering, and business teams.

Data systems
BigQuery and SQL-heavy work across pipelines, data marts, dashboard-ready models, GA4 export structures, and cost-conscious redesign. Python used for analysis, API extraction, and automation.

Hands-on funnel analysis, feature adoption measurement, e-commerce event modelling, retention analysis, experimentation support, and product telemetry design across web and app environments.

Metric definitions, data contracts, implementation requirements, QA paths, data dictionaries, and documentation that help teams use the same numbers in the same way.
Stakeholder translation
The work often sits between engineering, product, analytics, marketing, and leadership: enough technical detail to be useful, enough business context to change decisions.
