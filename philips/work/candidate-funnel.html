Case study · Path 02 · Instrumentation & Cross-Platform Telemetry

A 50%+ candidate drop-off, decomposed step by step.

Front-end behavioural instrumentation turned a vague sourcing problem into a step-level funnel question, replacing a manual Excel workflow with a GA4-to-BigQuery Looker Studio dashboard and changing how stakeholders investigated candidate loss.

/ Global AI services firm · candidate sourcing platform · 2025

Role-fit signal
Funnel instrumentation as measurement design. The Philips brief asks for event definitions that translate data needs into dashboard-ready telemetry. This case shows that translation in full: from a vague 50%+ drop-off problem to per-step abandon rates, user-identity parameters, and a queryable Looker Studio view that changed the conversation before it changed the product.
→
What happened
A sourcing team moved from “we’re losing candidates somewhere” to “step 4 has a 43% abandonment rate; let’s look at what’s happening there.” The measurement layer made the candidate journey decomposable for the first time.
The funnel can only be fixed once the steps have been made visible. Instrumentation is not the answer — it is the condition for having a question worth investigating.
Situation
A global AI services firm’s sourcing team knew there was a 50%+ drop-off between candidate selection and start of work, but could not locate the loss. Metrics lived in hand-populated Excel spreadsheets. No step-level funnel view existed.
Task
Instrument the candidate journey across application start, exam steps, compliance checkpoints, and selection status — with user-identity parameters enabling per-candidate segmentation. Present the work as new data available for decisions, not a premature conclusion to react to.
Action
GA4 event capture led with two web engineers. User-identity parameters enabled per-candidate and per-programme segmentation. A Looker Studio dashboard on the GA4-to-BigQuery pipeline replaced the manual Excel workflow with a queryable funnel view across all stages.
Result
Step-four abandonment surfaced at 43%. The manual Excel workflow was replaced. An analytics roadmap was built and presented for three product areas: Candidate Sourcing Platform, Refer a Friend, and My Performance Dashboard.
