Section 01 / selected work three engagements / three different problem shapes / fifteen more on work

Case / Question formulation

Consumer electronics retailer / 2023-2025 / under NDA

A workaround broke. The data confessed.

I was building a lifecycle segmentation model when an anomaly caught my eye: a cohort returning shortly after purchase to buy specific components. A Sense-Making conversation with floor staff surfaced the cause: a shadow IT compatibility tool had quietly stopped working.

Most interesting findings in analytics arrive while you are doing something else. The role of instrumentation is to make the gap visible enough that the question becomes worth asking.

The client subsequently shipped a compatibility configurator. It is now central to the business, with a second iteration underway.

Case / Adoption

State-regulated retail platform / 2025-2026

The system outlived the team that built it.

Greenfield UX telemetry across web, app, and CRM, plus a three-session structured handoff so the client could maintain and extend it independently. They did — when the full team rolled off in June 2025, incoming analysts were operational on day one. The system has been running independently since March 2026.

The 38 hours recovered per month is not the point. The point is what those hours got redirected to, and that the redirection survived the people who started it.

Case / Methodology / UX research

National home services franchise / Stanley Steemer / 2021-2022

Telemetry locates. Method diagnoses.

Quantitative GA4 telemetry paired with a UserZoom protocol built on Dervin's neutral-questioning. The funnel knew where users dropped off. It could not say what kind of gap they were experiencing.

A winning A/B variant was winning despite a vocabulary problem, not because of clean messaging. Optimising on the quant signal alone would have entrenched the wrong message.

This is the case behind the talk "Finding the Hypothesis When You Only Know the Question," and the methodology piece the Sense-Making references point back to.

Section 03 / approach the working position

Approach

Behaviour is the most honest data source available.

People make their problems visible through their actions. The doctor who builds a workaround. The leadership team that keeps requesting the same report in five different formats because the underlying question has never been answered.

Principle 01

Start with the question.

Before implementation: what decision are you trying to make, and what is the data not doing for you?

Principle 02

Build for adoption.

The job is done when the people around the system can use it independently.

Principle 03

Work across the boundary.

Instrumentation and qualitative method belong together when the measurement layer needs to surface human questions.
