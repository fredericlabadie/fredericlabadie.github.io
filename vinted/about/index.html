About · Vinted Marketing Modelling

Senior analytics work, pointed at marketing measurement.

I am a data and analytics leader based in Amsterdam with 14 years of experience across marketing analytics, product analytics, data architecture, and stakeholder enablement. The common thread is translating unclear business questions into data systems that people can trust and use — and being explicit about what the numbers can and cannot claim.

For Vinted, the relevant part is the operating pattern this role describes: build the measurement, advise the people spending the budget, and define the problem carefully before modelling it. The media mix model at Stanley Steemer is the closest single proof — a production model whose output changed real budget decisions, built on an identity layer engineered first.

What transfers

Profile

The transferable pattern is measurement plus the advisory judgement on top of it.

Marketing modelling
A production media mix model: identity resolution via hash blocking and graph clustering, Census household saturation, a regression base for addressable channels, and a stakeholder-agreed weighted-allocation layer for passive ones.
Experimentation
A/B testing introduced as a leadership decision-making practice — hypothesis through statistical evaluation, and the culture change that made leaders request evidence before decisions.
Canonical definitions
Driving alignment toward one customer definition rather than accommodating several: a customer data model, event schemas, and segmentation logic held canonical through a live CDP migration.
Data engineering
BigQuery pipeline architecture, SQL redesign, and cost discipline — the engineering that keeps model inputs trustworthy and canonical logic defined once.

Skills

From identity resolution to a budget recommendation leadership can defend.

Statistical modelling
Linear regression for channel attribution, applied Naive Bayes analysis, weighted allocation design for unaddressable channels, and A/B significance evaluation communicated in decision-grade terms.
Identity and audience data
Multi-attribute hash blocking, union-find graph clustering, householding, Census/ZCTA integration, and customer data model design across CDP and warehouse environments.
Data engineering and BI
BigQuery, SQL pipelines, Python for analysis and API extraction, dbt Fundamentals, Dataform, R prototyping against SQL Server, Knime, Looker Studio as primary BI environment, and Tableau.
Stakeholder advisory
Translating model output into budget recommendations, agreeing method scope openly with stakeholders, and framing findings — including negative ones — so they change what the organisation does next.
