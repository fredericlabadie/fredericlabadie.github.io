The strongest overlap between the role and the portfolio, stated as three claims. Each is backed by a full case page below, with tools, method, and results.

01 · A media mix model that changed real budget decisions
A production MMM at Stanley Steemer: an engineered identity-resolution layer under a linear-regression base for addressable channels, plus a stakeholder-agreed allocation layer for passive channels. The sharpest output was a falsifiable negative finding that redirected the direct mail programme.
02 · Experimentation as a leadership practice
Introduced A/B testing to leadership operating on decades of instinct. By year two, leaders were requesting evidence before decisions rather than after — the culture change Vinted's own team describes as “steering with actual math”.
03 · One canonical definition of the customer
Drove alignment toward a single customer data model through a live CDP migration at a consumer electronics retailer — event schemas and segmentation logic redesigned, not copied forward, with zero campaign downtime.
Context that transfers
The privacy shift that returned media mix modelling to the centre of the measurement stack is familiar from the instrumentation side: GDPR and consent implementation across three organisations, with OneTrust and TrustArc in production. The measurement history is US consumer marketing — Census and ZCTA joins, USPS address data, direct-mail economics — relevant context for a marketplace expanding into that market. And the householding layer under the Stanley Steemer model is the same structure that makes per-customer acquisition cost and lifetime value computable.

Foundations in hand.

dbt
Dataform in production — staged SQL, dependency graphs, tested outputs, the same transformation model dbt runs — plus dbt Fundamentals certification (April 2026). The SQL and data-modelling foundation transfers directly.
BI tooling
Tableau and Looker Studio, on years of BI delivery across SSRS and warehouse-backed reporting. The Looker platform itself is the ramp item: its semantic-layer concepts map onto modelling work already done elsewhere.
Bayesian methods
Graduate-level Bayesian data analysis in Python through the MSc, alongside time-series analysis in Python and regression work in R, and working fluency in the adstock, saturation, and uncertainty-quantification mechanics that make Bayesian MMM the right tool for confounded channel data. The production model in the Stanley Steemer case used a linear-regression base with a stakeholder-agreed allocation layer; PyMC is a fast build on that foundation.
