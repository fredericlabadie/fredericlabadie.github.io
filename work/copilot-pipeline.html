Case study · Lens 07 · Data Engineering · AI platform analytics

Front-end behavioural signal for a commercial Claude-powered platform.

Adding the front-end measurement layer to an enterprise platform that had only backend metering for billing.

/ Enterprise AI copilot platform · agency-client engagement

→
What happened
An enterprise AI copilot platform built on Anthropic's Claude gained a full front-end behavioural signal layer — GA4 event collection and BigQuery export covering platform-wide and org-specific events (which copilots are being used, how often, by whom). Pipeline confirmed live and in use by engineering teams within weeks of delivery. Continued afterwards as internal BigQuery SME, including a GA4-querying primer for the engineering team, plus advisory input to the platform's adversarial-testing tool on its own measurement layer.
Context
An enterprise AI copilot platform built on Anthropic's Claude had scaled from internal tool to commercial product but had only backend metering for billing. No front-end behavioural signal layer existed — the engineering team couldn't answer which copilots were being used, by whom, how often, or what shape user behaviour was taking inside the product.
What I did
Joined as Analytics Architect for front-end measurement. Designed and delivered the full GA4 event collection and BigQuery export pipeline — platform-wide and org-specific behavioural events. Pipeline live and in use within weeks. Then served as internal BigQuery SME including a querying primer for the engineering team beginning to work with the data (unnesting event parameters, schema model, KPI queries: total copilot count, conversations per user, Custom Copilot access-type distribution). Later, advisory input to the platform's adversarial-testing tool on its own measurement layer.
