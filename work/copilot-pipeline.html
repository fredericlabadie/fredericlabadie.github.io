Case study · Topic 07 · Data Engineering · AI platform analytics

Front-end behavioural signal for a commercial Claude-powered platform.

Adding the front-end measurement layer to an enterprise platform that had only backend metering for billing.

/ Enterprise AI copilot platform · agency-client engagement

An enterprise AI copilot platform built on Anthropic's Claude scaled from internal tool to commercial product, but had only backend metering for billing — no front-end behavioural signal layer. Joined as Analytics Architect for front-end measurement: designed and delivered the full GA4 event collection and BigQuery export pipeline covering platform-wide and org-specific behavioural events (which copilots are being used, how often, by whom). Pipeline confirmed live and in use by engineering teams within weeks of delivery.

After delivery, served as the internal BigQuery subject-matter expert — including a primer on querying GA4 data (unnesting event parameters, schema model, KPI queries: total copilot count, conversations per user, Custom Copilot access-type distribution) for the engineering team beginning to work with the data. Later provided advisory input to the platform's adversarial-testing tool on its own measurement layer.

GA4 → BigQuery Looker Studio Enterprise AI Engineering enablement
