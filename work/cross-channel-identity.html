Case study · Topic 06 · Marketing Analytics · Cross-channel identity

Connecting email behaviour to in-app sessions.

The instrumentation backbone behind every "did this campaign actually drive product interaction or just a click?" question.

/ State-regulated retail platform · Braze + GA4 + web

Authored the spec to push Braze ID and Account GUID into the GA data layer, and to carry Braze ID in email URL parameters — defining the cross-channel identity stitching that connects CRM behaviour (email opens, clicks) to app and web sessions for a unified user-journey view.

Same engagement also covered GTM audit, consent-aware tag configuration under TrustArc, and recurring monthly readout cycles producing executive summaries of user behaviour and product engagement trends.
