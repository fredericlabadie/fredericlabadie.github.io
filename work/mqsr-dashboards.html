Case study · Lens 03 · Experimentation · Campaign analytics

A campaign dashboard, a schema decomposition, and a North Star that holds.

Extending an existing Amplitude architecture — with a schema decomposition that unlocked the analysis the mixed field had been hiding.

/ Major quick-service restaurant · Amplitude · North Star (Transactions)

→
What happened
A major QSR's Amplitude measurement architecture (anchored on a Transactions North Star) gained a BOGO seasonal-promotion dashboard with full deal-conversion funnel, year-over-year comparison, loyalty-acquisition lift, and platform-level error breakdown — the last of which surfaced an Android-isolated error spike that was invisible in the aggregate. Same engagement also proposed a decomposition of an overloaded products.variant property into five discrete sub-properties, unlocking revenue-by-size, upsell attachment, customisation, and combo-penetration analysis that the original mixed field had made impossible.
Context
A major quick-service restaurant with an existing Amplitude architecture anchored on a Transactions North Star (Order Completed + Store Loyalty Checkin Completed + Receipt Scan Checkin Completed) and a BOGO seasonal promotion to measure. Two problems sat alongside: the existing dashboards couldn't decompose platform-level error patterns, and an overloaded products.variant property was hiding revenue-by-size, upsell attachment, customisation, and combo-penetration analysis.
What I did
Built the BOGO seasonal-promotion dashboard with full deal-conversion funnel, year-over-year comparison, loyalty-acquisition lift analysis, and platform-level error breakdown — the last of which surfaced an Android-isolated error spike invisible in the aggregate. Separately proposed the decomposition of products.variant into five discrete sub-properties, unlocking the analyses the mixed field had made impossible.
