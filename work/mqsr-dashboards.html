Case study · Topic 03 · Experimentation · Campaign analytics

A campaign dashboard, a schema decomposition, and a North Star that holds.

Extending an existing Amplitude architecture — with a schema decomposition that unlocked the analysis the mixed field had been hiding.

/ Major quick-service restaurant · Amplitude · North Star (Transactions)

Extended an existing Amplitude measurement architecture anchored on a Transactions North Star (Order Completed + Store Loyalty Checkin Completed + Receipt Scan Checkin Completed). Built a BOGO seasonal-promotion dashboard with full deal-conversion funnel, year-over-year comparison, loyalty-acquisition lift analysis, and platform-level error breakdown that surfaced an Android-isolated error spike invisible in the aggregate.

Separately proposed a decomposition of an overloaded products.variant property into five discrete sub-properties — unlocking revenue-by-size, upsell attachment, customisation, and combo penetration analysis that the original mixed field made impossible.
