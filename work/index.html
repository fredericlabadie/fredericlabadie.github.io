Topic 01

Question Formulation & Discovery

Before any instrumentation, the right question. The cases below show what Sense-Making questioning and structured discovery surface that data alone cannot.

For: UX Telemetry · Insight · Product Strategy

Case · Methodology piece National home services franchise (Stanley Steemer, anonymised) · 2021–2022

Mixed-Methods UX Research — diagnosing gap type, not just gap location.

GA4/A-B telemetry locating where users fell out, paired with a UserZoom protocol that was ~90% SMM-informed neutral questioning. The protocol surfaced findings the funnel could not — including that "Promo Code" and "Online Specials" were read as categorically different things, and that professional cleaning sits in users' sense-making as a treat, not maintenance. The argument: analytics and qualitative research are not competing methods — they are sequential ones.
