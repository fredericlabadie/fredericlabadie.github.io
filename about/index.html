Bio

Analyst, architect, and the person you call when the data won't behave.

Fourteen years building the analytics layer that lets organisations stop guessing — data models, tracking instrumentation, KPI frameworks, dashboards, and the experimentation culture that makes any of it useful.

Most recently as Analytics Architect at Telus Digital, working across enterprise clients in consumer electronics, B2B SaaS, e-commerce, and travel. Before that, five years at Stanley Steemer building marketing analytics from scratch across 280 franchise locations — greenfield, BigQuery-backed BI on the way out, A/B testing culture introduced and adopted at the leadership level.

I've worked under HIPAA on protected data, alongside enterprise consent/privacy regimes including OneTrust and TrustArc. My usability research practice is built on Sense-Making Methodology paired with the standard practitioner toolkit (task-based testing, think-aloud protocols, heuristic evaluation, SUS / task-success metrics) — the qualitative side of the work, not an afterthought to the quantitative side.

I'm drawn to high-stakes contexts because stress and constraint strip away performance. What remains is signal. I work with teams that want their measurement layer to surface the human question underneath the metric — not just confirm what they already believe.

Based in Amsterdam. EU work authorisation. Currently FTE; open to the right next role with standard notice.
